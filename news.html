To G.C.E A/l students

Students who will be sitting for A/L’s in 2022 & 2023, The school has arranged to give you your COVID-19 vaccine Tomorrow, on the 22nd of October 2021. The program will take place from 7.30 a.m. to 01.30 p.m. All students should enter the school via the Primary Gate. It is compulsory for all students to bring their SRB & NIC with them. Students of the 2022 A/L batch should bring their grade 12 SRB, 2023 A/L students should bring their grade 11 SRB. All students should come in school uniform, clean shaven and with a proper haircut.

To G.C.E A/l students

Dear Students,
Applications for the G.C.E. Advanced Level admissions to The Royal College are open now. More details on the requirements and subject stream selection for admissions are detailed in the admissions page. Stay Safe Thank You.
